    Internship Experience! ðŸ’¼

      I fulfilled my internship at Pejabat Tanah dan Daerah Kecil Rengit. In Pejabat Tanah Rengit, there are 3 departments, which are the Administrative and Technical Department, the Revenue Counter Department, and the Registration Department, I was placed in the Registration Department with my 3 other friends. Here is the complete photo of the Internship Student in July until August batch.

      In the registration department we do the matter of registering client info regarding their land. Some of the tasks that I do while my internship is issuing "Carian Rasmi", "Salinan Sah", "Kutipan Geran" and many others. As for me, it's not an easy responsibility, moreover, it involves such a piece of confidential information regarding clients and their assets. Alhamdulillah, I managed to complete the daily task in the Registration Department with guidance from my officers, Mr. Aminnuddin and Mrs. Zuraida.

      Not forgetting, even when I already finished my internship, Pejabat Tanah Rengit's staff still invited me to join their Family Day. I'm so happy and grateful to spend such a wise time with them during Family Day. Overall, to be honest, it was quite a tough but exciting experience all along I did my internship at the Pejabat Tanah Rengit! I have learned so many things along my internship journey and most of them were a beautiful moment. With all of the knowledge that I gained, I am grateful and I hope this internship journey will make myself to be a more responsible and knowledgeable person, In Shaa Allah.
